About Chimtu

Chimtu is a friendly reminder bot designed to make your day healthier and more productive. Whether you're working at a desk, studying, or just relaxing, Chimtu sends smart reminders through Telegram to help you stay hydrated, focused, and on schedule.

Created with care and designed to delight, Chimtu is your cute companion that actually cares. Its OLED eyes, compact body, and buzzer alerts make it not just functional, but lovable too.
